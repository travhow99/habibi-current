About Habibi Hookah Lounge and Cafe

Opened in 1997, Habibi Hookah Cafe is the oldest hookah lounge in all of Colorado. While ownership has changed, the Broadway location remains the same iconic hookah stop, just now with a little added flair. Grab your friends and join us and our DJ on the weekends, or come by to study over a nice cup of tea or coffee.

Broadway Location

In 2016 Habibi expanded to a second location in Parker, which delivers a high-class hookah experience with the same quality and service expected with the Habibi name. Both locations feature the largest selection of top-quality shisha available, with specially priced hookahs before 7.

Parker Location

Our Selection

Both Habibi locations carry the finest selection of hookahs and shisha available. With hookahs ranging from $20 up, Habibi has the right hookah pipe for you. Habibi can also meet your hookah repair needs, or provide many different customizations.

Habibi sells many brands of shisha including Starbuzz, Puff Wiser, Al Fakher, and more. Stop by today for a suggestion or to try a new flavor!
