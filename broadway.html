Habibi On Broadway

Habibi Hookah Cafe is the perfect place for hookah smokers seeking a relaxed atmosphere during the day, and a lively atmosphere at night--complete with music, dancing, and DJs on the weekends.

We also have the best brands of tobacco, hookahs, and vapor products and supplies for sale. Current brands we serve/sell: Al Fakher, Star Buzz, Haze, Tangiers, Fumari, Social Smoke, Fantasia, and Argelini. We can also repair or customize any hookah you can bring us, we're up for the challenge!

We opened in early 2004 on South Broadway with a small staff, unsure of what the hookah business would have in store for us. Now, several years and a change in ownership later, our staff and regulars are a family, and the hookah nightlife continues to be as busy, fast-paced, and high-spirited as ever. Over the years, we've seen and done it all, in a rich cultural setting that doubles as a night club. Come see us and experience our rich history for yourselves!
